WE KEEP GEETING STRONGER

The India chapter of GRAVITY Gym started in 2002, when the first GRAVITY Gym India branch
was set up in JALANDHAR. In the next few years this number grew, and today GRAVITY Gym
has cut out for itself 120 gyms in India with another 5 under construction and ready to start in this year.

With multiple awards for excellence under its belt including 2 awards from the Lions Club for the “Best Fitness Chain”,
An award from the Images Beauty & wellness for the Most admired fitness chain of the year”, an award from
Franchise India, 8 awards from GRAVITY Gym International making it a very eventful 14 years in the growth of the business.
GRAVITY Gym India has bagged the most prestigious Economic Times ET award for ‘Excellence in Marketing 2013’
& ‘Excellence in Customer Service 2014’.
